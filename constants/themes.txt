THEME: Minimal Black & White Ecommerce UI (Flipkart-inspired)

PRIMARY COLORS
- Pure White: #FFFFFF (Base background)
- Pure Black: #000000 (Primary text, borders, icons)

GRAY SCALE (Opacity-driven, no harsh colors)
- Gray 900: #111111 (Headings, strong text)
- Gray 800: #1A1A1A (Card titles)
- Gray 700: #2A2A2A (Secondary text)
- Gray 600: #3A3A3A (Muted labels)
- Gray 500: #5A5A5A (Descriptions)
- Gray 400: #8A8A8A (Disabled text)
- Gray 300: #CFCFCF (Borders, dividers)
- Gray 200: #E6E6E6 (Card outlines)
- Gray 100: #F4F4F4 (Section background)
- Gray 050: #FAFAFA (Soft background tint)

OPACITY USAGE (IMPORTANT)
- Primary text: rgba(0,0,0,0.95)
- Secondary text: rgba(0,0,0,0.72)
- Meta text: rgba(0,0,0,0.55)
- Disabled text: rgba(0,0,0,0.35)
- Borders: rgba(0,0,0,0.12)
- Card shadow: rgba(0,0,0,0.08)

COMPONENT STYLING
- Cards: White background, 1px subtle border, no heavy shadows
- Hover: Border darkens slightly + 2–4% scale or lift
- Buttons:
  - Primary: Black bg, White text
  - Secondary: White bg, Black border, Black text
- Badges: Gray 100 bg, Gray 800 text

LAYOUT & FEEL
- Flat design (no gradients)
- Rounded corners: 12–16px
- Generous spacing (Flipkart-like airy grid)
- Strong visual hierarchy using font weight, not color

ICON & IMAGE TREATMENT
- Icons: Monochrome (black/gray)
- Product images: Floating on white, soft shadow only

MOOD
- Editorial
- Premium
- Calm
- Trust-focused
